WHAT WAS IMPLEMENTED
====================
A full-page analytics view at /analytics/categories that ranks all expense categories
by total spend. Shows a summary header (total spend, category count, top category)
and a ranked list with icon, name, amount, transaction count, percentage of total,
and a proportional colour-coded progress bar per category.
